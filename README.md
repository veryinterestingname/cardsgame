Welcome to blackjack!

Objective: beat the dealer's hand by either:
1. getting a higher total value than the dealer without exceeding 21
2. have the dealer exceed 21

card values:
number cards (2-10) are worth their face value

face cards (J, Q, K) are all worth 10

Aces are worth either 1 or 11 (The game automatically chooses the value that gives you the highest score without busting)

How to play
Before any cards are dealt, you must place a bet. You can change your bet with the + and - buttons, then start the hand

You will reeceive two cards face up, the dealer will receive two cards, one face up, and one face down.

Once the cards are dealt, you can choose one of the following actions:
1. Hit: Draw another card
2. Stand: Keep your current hand and end your turn
3. Double (Double Down): Only possible on your first two cards (i.e. before you hit or stand), doubles your bet and gives you exactly one more card, then ends your turn
4. Split: Only possible if your first two cards have the same value (e.g. two 8s), splits them into two separate hands and places an additional bet equal to your original bet. You play the two hands separately and independently of each other.

Dealer's turn:

After you finish your turn(s) without busting, the dealer reveals their hidden card, then:

the dealer must hit if their total is 16 or lower

the dealer must stand if their total is 17 or higher

If your total is higher than the dealer's you win, your bet is doubled, and added to your balance

If you bust (go over 21) or if the dealer's total is higher than yours, you lose your bet.

If you and the dealer have the same total, it's a tie, and your bet is returned to you with no additional money.

The game keeps going until you have 0$ in your bank account, at which point you can choose to play again, starting with 1000$ like you did at first
